Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 6, 2026

Daily ("we", "our", "the app") is an AI-powered day planner. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

What We Collect

  • Account information — When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from Google OAuth. We use this solely for authentication.
  • Search queries — The cities and preferences you enter are sent to our server to generate your day plan. We do not store your queries after the response is delivered.
  • Local storage — The app caches geocoding results, media data, and your preferences (theme, last city) in your browser's local storage. This data stays on your device.

What We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your location automatically. You type in the city you want to explore.
  • We do not sell, share, or transfer your personal data to third parties for advertising or marketing.
  • We do not track you across other websites or apps.
  • We do not collect data from children under 13. The app is not directed at children.

Third-Party Services

The app uses these external services to function:

  • Google OAuth — for sign-in (Google Privacy Policy)
  • Anthropic (Claude) — to generate day plans (queries are processed by Anthropic's API)
  • OpenStreetMap / Nominatim — for geocoding places on the map
  • Wikipedia / Wikimedia Commons — for place images
  • YouTube — for place video recommendations (searched server-side)
  • Vercel — for hosting (Vercel Privacy Policy)

How We Share, Transfer, or Disclose Google User Data

When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture. This Google user data may be shared with the following service providers solely to operate the app:

  • Supabase — stores your authentication session and account record (name, email, profile picture) so you can stay signed in. (Supabase Privacy Policy)
  • Stripe — receives your email address to process payments and manage your subscription. (Stripe Privacy Policy)
  • Vercel — hosts the application; your requests (including authentication tokens) pass through Vercel's infrastructure. (Vercel Privacy Policy)

We do not sell, rent, or share your Google user data with any other third parties. We do not use your Google user data for advertising, analytics profiling, or any purpose beyond providing the app's core functionality (authentication, payment processing, and hosting).

Data Protection

We take the following measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption in transit — All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS.
  • Encryption at rest — Account data stored in Supabase is encrypted at rest using AES-256 encryption provided by the hosting infrastructure.
  • Minimal data collection — We only collect the data strictly necessary to provide the service. We do not store search queries or generated plans on our servers.
  • Access controls — Access to production systems and databases is restricted to the app administrator and protected by strong authentication.
  • Secure authentication — We use industry-standard OAuth 2.0 via Google for sign-in. We never receive or store your Google password.

Data Retention

We do not maintain a database of user queries or generated plans. Browser-side caches (local storage) can be cleared at any time through your browser settings.

Your Choices

  • You can use the app without signing in (with limited features).
  • You can clear local storage at any time to remove cached data.
  • You can revoke Google sign-in access from your Google Account settings.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the "last updated" date above.
